How loud are you?

Settings

These parameters are rough estimates based on my personal testing. Since every microphohne is different, you'll likely need to test out several different values for the noise levels until you find what works right.

To get started, I'd recommend running the app and keeping an eye on the values. What are the numbers when things are good, what are they when they're ok, and what are they when they're just too loud? Remember, you can always change them!

This site doesn't collect any personal info, or save any recordings. If it's not working, please make sure you allowed it to access your microphone. Again, nothing is being recorded, so it's safe. If you'd like to verify yourself, see the code on github.
